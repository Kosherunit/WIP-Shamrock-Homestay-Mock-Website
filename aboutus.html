About Us

Discover the story behind Shamrock Homes

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Shamrock Homes was born from a dream to create a sanctuary where travelers could experience the true beauty of Batu Ferringhi's beachfront paradise.

What began as a private family retreat gradually evolved into an exclusive homestay destination, carefully preserving the intimate atmosphere while offering world-class amenities and service.

Our beachfront location provides guests with direct access to one of Penang's most beautiful stretches of coastline, while still maintaining proximity to local attractions and cultural experiences.

Our Mission

At Shamrock Homes, our mission is to provide an exceptional guest experience that captures the essence of Malaysian hospitality while offering modern comforts and amenities.

We strive to create a home away from home where guests can disconnect from the stresses of everyday life and reconnect with nature, loved ones, and themselves.

Authentic Hospitality

We welcome every guest like family, with genuine warmth and personalized service.

Environmental Stewardship

We are committed to sustainable practices that preserve the natural beauty of our surroundings.

Excellence in Service

We continuously strive to exceed expectations in every aspect of our service.

About the Creator

Mr Ivan Lee

Founder/Producer

With over 15 years of experience in the Content creation industry and a tech enthusiast, Ivan Lee founded Shamrock Homes with a vision to create a unique beachfront experience that combines luxury and authenticity.

Born and raised in Penang, Ivan has a deep connection to the local culture and landscape. His passion for hospitality was cultivated during his years working with international hotel chains across Southeast Asia.

"I wanted to create a place where guests could experience the real Penang – its natural beauty, rich culture, and warm hospitality – while enjoying the comforts and conveniences of modern accommodation," says Ivan.

When not overseeing operations at Shamrock Homes, Ivan enjoys exploring new culinary experiences, supporting local conservation efforts, and spending time with his family.
